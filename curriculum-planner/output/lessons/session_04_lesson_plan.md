**Theme:** Pacing Overview
**Duration:** 450 minutes
**Materials:** Laptops, projector, student handouts

## LEARNING TARGETS

1. Session 4 Contribution _(Bloom's: Apply)_
2. By the end of Session 4, students should be able to: _(Bloom's: Apply)_
3. Present a complete data analysis to a real audience with confidence in English (LO #7) _(Bloom's: Apply)_
4. Defend methodology and conclusions under live questioning (LO #7) _(Bloom's: Evaluate)_
5. Assemble a professional data portfolio demonstrating the full analysis pipeline (LO #3, #6) _(Bloom's: Apply)_
6. Articulate their data literacy growth from Day 1 to Day 4 (metacognition) _(Bloom's: Apply)_
7. Position data skills professionally on LinkedIn (career readiness) _(Bloom's: Apply)_
8. Cumulative Progression _(Bloom's: Apply)_
9. Summary of All Timing Adjustments _(Bloom's: Apply)_
10. Total bootcamp duration standardized to 28 contact hours across 4 sessions. Sessions 1–2 extended from 5:30 to 5:45. Session 1 gains a 30-minute buffer block for tool troubleshooting. All other adjustments are internal rebalancing. _(Bloom's: Apply)_
